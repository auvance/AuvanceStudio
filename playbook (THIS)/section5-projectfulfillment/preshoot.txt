<aside>

Every shoot is unique, so customize this checklist based on your equipment, client needs, and project requirements. Keeping a printed or digital version ensures nothing is overlooked.

</aside>

---

### **1. Equipment Preparation**

- [ ]  **Camera(s)**: Fully charged and tested.
- [ ]  **Lenses**: Pack all necessary lenses (wide-angle, zoom, prime, etc.).
- [ ]  **Memory Cards**: Format and ensure sufficient storage.
- [ ]  **Batteries**: Fully charged and pack spares.
- [ ]  **Tripod/Monopod**: Test stability.
- [ ]  **Lighting Equipment**: Softboxes, LED panels, reflectors, light stands.
- [ ]  **Audio Equipment**:
    - [ ]  Microphones (lapel, shotgun, etc.).
    - [ ]  Audio recorder or external mic setup.
    - [ ]  Extra batteries for audio devices.
- [ ]  **Drones/Gimbals** (if applicable): Fully charged and operational.
- [ ]  **Cables and Adapters**: HDMI, USB-C, power cords, etc.
- [ ]  **Backup Equipment**: Spare camera body or secondary gear.

---

### **2. Personal Preparation**

- [ ]  **Wardrobe**: Dress appropriately for the shoot environment.
- [ ]  **Snacks/Water**: Stay hydrated and energized.
- [ ]  **Comfort Gear**: Sunscreen, hats, or weather-appropriate clothing.
- [ ]  **Notebook or Tablet**: For notes and shoot references.

---

### **3. Shoot Planning**

- [ ]  **Shot List**: Printed or on your device.
- [ ]  **Storyboard/Concepts**: Any sketches or inspiration references.
- [ ]  **Client Expectations**: Review client notes, emails, or briefs.
- [ ]  **Permissions**: Any location or shoot permits ready.

---

### **4. Client Deliverables**

- [ ]  **Branded Items**: Any props or materials showcasing the client's brand.
- [ ]  **Pre-loaded Media**: Any pre-shot clips or assets for reference.
- [ ]  **Contracts/Agreements**: Ensure they're signed and accessible.

---

### **5. Test Equipment**

- [ ]  **Camera Settings**: Adjust ISO, shutter speed, and white balance.
- [ ]  **Audio Check**: Test microphones and monitor levels.
- [ ]  **Lighting Test**: Ensure proper lighting for the space.

---

### **6. Safety and Backup**

- [ ]  **Hard Drive/SSD**: For backing up footage immediately.
- [ ]  **Rain Covers/Weather Gear**: Protection for gear.
- [ ]  **First Aid Kit**: For minor injuries.

5. Project Fulfillment
