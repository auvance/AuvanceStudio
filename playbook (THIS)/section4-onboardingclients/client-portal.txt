<aside>

### Customization

</aside>

The **Client Portal** is not just about organization—it's an opportunity to showcase your brand and create a personalized experience for your client.

**Branding Your Portal:**

- Add your **logo and** brand colors to make the portal align with your business identity.
- Customize the layout to match your style while maintaining the clean and professional aesthetic that makes a great first impression.

**Personalizing for the Client:**

- Include **images or visuals** from the client’s business, such as photos of their property, logo, or products.
- Tailor the **project overview** to reflect their specific goals and key deliverables.
- Name the portal uniquely, e.g., "[Client Name] Project Hub," to create a sense of exclusivity and collaboration.

<aside>

### How to Use and Duplicate the Notion Client Portal

</aside>

Follow these steps to access and duplicate the Notion Client Portal template into your own Notion workspace:

1. **Open the Template Link**
    - Click on the provided Notion link to open the template. This will open the portal in your web browser or Notion app.
2. **Log In to Notion**
    - Make sure you are logged into your Notion account. If you don’t have one, you can sign up for free at notion.so.
3. **Duplicate the Template**
    - In the top-right corner of the page, click the "**Duplicate**" button.
    - Select the workspace where you'd like to save the duplicated template.
4. **Customize the Portal**
    - Once the template is in your workspace, edit it to match your brand by adding your logo, brand colors, and fonts.
    - Update the project overview, deliverables, and client details to reflect the specific project.
5. **Share with Your Client**
    - When the portal is ready, click "**Share**" in the top-right corner.
    - Toggle "**Share to Web**" and copy the link to send to your client. You can also manage permissions to ensure they can view (but not edit) the portal.
