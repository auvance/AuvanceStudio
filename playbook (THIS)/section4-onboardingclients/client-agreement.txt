<aside>

The **client agreement** is a vital document designed to protect both you and your clients by outlining the terms and conditions of your collaboration. It ensures clarity, accountability, and legal protection for both parties as you move forward with the project.

💡 **Note:**

This agreement template was originally designed for a video/content business, but the structure applies to any creative service.

You’ll need to adapt sections like deliverables, timelines, and usage rights to match your specific service (design, branding, social media, etc.).

</aside>

<aside>

For E-Signatures: 

- Download the agreement as a PDF once edited & ready
- Upload the PDF to your software of choice (I recommend Docusign or PandaDoc)
- Add date and signature fields, then send it to the client for signing.
</aside>

---

<aside>

### Key Notes

</aside>

- **Legal Protection:** This agreement was crafted with professional legal advice, ensuring it covers essential points for a service-based business. However, as laws vary by country, it’s your responsibility to confirm its validity in your location. If needed, consult a local lawyer to adapt it to your specific legal requirements.
- **Customizing:** Edit the template to reflect your services, deliverables, pricing structure, and unique offerings. ****This ensures the agreement aligns with your business model and the client’s expectations.
- **Governing Law:** At the end of the agreement, specify the governing law of your country or region to solidify its legal standing.

This agreement is a foundation of trust and professionalism, designed to give both you and your clients peace of mind.

Never skip the agreement - even for small or “easy” projects. Clear terms upfront prevent problems later.
