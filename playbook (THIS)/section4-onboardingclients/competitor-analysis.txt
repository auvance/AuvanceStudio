Win trust from day one by showing clients you understand their industry — and how to help them stand out.

<aside>

The **competitor analysis** template is a strategic document designed to give your clients clarity on where they stand in their market and how your work will help them stand out in their market. It positions you as a strategic partner, not just a service provider.

💡 **Note:**

This template was originally created with a video/content-focused approach, but the structure applies to any creative service.

Adapt the analysis to your field—whether that’s branding, design, social media, copywriting, or another creative discipline.

</aside>

---

<aside>

### Key Notes

</aside>

- **Client Trust:** This is one of the best ways to build trust right from the onboarding stage. By analyzing and presenting ow competitors are performing in their market, you're showing initiative and deep understanding of the client’s industry.
- **Customization:** The document is fully editable to match your client’s niche. You can swap in direct competitors, adjust branding, and include platform-specific or channel-specific observations (Instagram, TikTok, websites, ads, email, etc.)
- **Clear Positioning:** This is a foundation to show how your approach will stand out. Use it to highlight gaps in the market and justify your recommendations in a way that’s backed by insight.

Most creatives jump straight into execution.
When you bring strategy into the conversation, you immediately stand out.
